## 🌐 English Version

### **Project Overview**
**Travel Record** is a professional travel log management tool specifically designed for macOS. 

Developed by aviation enthusiasts, it turns your digital records into a professional **"Flight Logbook"** experience. Powered by Apple's latest **SwiftData** framework, it helps you track every flight and train journey with intelligent, time-based archiving.

---

### 🌟 **Key Features**

#### **1. Smart Trip Classification**
* **Upcoming**: Real-time monitoring. Trips automatically move to history the moment the departure time passes.
* **History**: A chronological archive of all your past travel memories.

#### **2. Professional Flight System**
* Designed by aviation fans to mimic a professional flight manual. 
* It’s open-source, lightweight, and focused on the details that matter to frequent flyers.

#### **3. Deep Railway Integration**
* Optimized for different national railway logics (e.g., China Railway seat classes like Business, First Class, and Sleepers). 
* Supports cascading selectors for Operators and Seat Types.

#### **4. Native macOS Experience**
* Built with SwiftUI to fit perfectly with the macOS aesthetic. 
* Supports standard inputs, date pickers, and native window behaviors.

#### **5. Data Privacy & Security**
* All data is stored locally via SwiftData. 
* No internet required, ensuring your travel history stays private and secure.

---

### 🛠️ **Technical Implementation**
* **Framework**: SwiftUI (Targeting macOS 14.0+)
* **Persistence**: SwiftData (Next-generation data modeling)
* **Auto-Refresh**: Driven by `Combine` timers to ensure UI accuracy.
* **Clean Formatting**: Optimized flight number inputs (no more "8,703" comma interference).

## 🇨🇳 中文版本

### **项目简介**
**Travel Record** 是一款专为 macOS 设计的个人旅行足迹管理工具。

本软件由专业飞友制作，致力于将数字记录打造为**“专业飞行手册”**般的体验。利用 Apple 最新的 **SwiftData** 技术，它可以帮助你记录每一次飞行和铁路旅程，并能根据时间自动归档行程。

---

### 🌟 **核心功能**

#### **1. 智能行程分类**
* **即将到来 (Upcoming)**：实时监控时间，一旦起飞/出发时间点已过，行程将自动移入历史记录。
* **历史足迹 (History)**：按照时间倒序排列你所有的旅行回忆。

#### **2. 详尽飞行系统**
* 由飞友为飞友制作，界面风格参考专业飞行手册。
* 完全免费开源，保持轻量与纯净。

#### **3. 深耕铁路系统**
* 特别针对不同国家的铁路逻辑进行优化（如中国铁路的席别：商务座、一等座、二等卧等）。
* 支持运营商与席位的级联选择（Country -> Company）。

#### **4. 原生 macOS 体验**
* 采用 SwiftUI 开发，完美契合 macOS 系统视觉风格。
* 支持标准表单输入、日期选择器等原生交互。

#### **5. 数据安全与隐私**
* 所有数据通过 SwiftData 存储在本地。
* 无需联网即可查看，隐私完全由你掌控。

---

### 🛠️ **技术实现**
* **核心框架**: SwiftUI (支持 macOS 14.0+)
* **数据持久化**: SwiftData 
* **自动刷新逻辑**: 使用 `Combine` 定时器驱动视图，确保状态随时间流逝自动切换。
* **数字格式化**: 针对航班号输入进行优化，去除了千分位逗号干扰，符合行业规范。
